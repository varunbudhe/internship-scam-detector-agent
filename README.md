# Internship Scam Detector Agent

## Problem
Many students and freshers waste time and money on fake internships, paid training scams, and unclear job offers.

## Solution
This AI agent analyzes job/internship offers and gives a structured decision.

## What it gives
- Scam Score
- Risk Level
- Red Flags
- Trust Signals
- Final Decision
- Next Action

## Why this matters
Before applying for any job, the first priority is safety and trust.
